About PaperSight
What it does

PaperSight photographs paper intake forms, extracts patient data using the Gemma 4 E2B vision model via Ollama, and assigns clinical triage priority based on WHO ETAT and SATS guidelines.

Key features
Fully offline — no cloud connectivity required
Works on any smartphone, tablet, or desktop browser
Rule-based triage — auditable and deterministic
Priority-sorted queue with red / yellow / green filtering
How to use
  1. Tap Photograph Intake Form and take a photo of the paper form
  2. Wait for extraction and triage scoring (20–30 seconds)
  3. Review the extracted data and priority in the results card
  4. The patient is automatically added to the triage queue
  5. Click any patient in the queue to view full details or delete
  6. Use filter tabs to view patients by priority level
